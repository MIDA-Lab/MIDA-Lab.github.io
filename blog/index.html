GVCFDiffusion: A Breakthrough for Seamless Large-Content Image Generation
Shoukun Sun PhD Student
March 06, 2025 March 19, 2025
diffusion-models text-to-image

Generating large images, such as panoramas or 360-degree scenes, remains a significant challenge in the field of text-to-image synthesis. Our research introduces Guided and Variance-Corrected Fusion with Style Alignment, a novel approach to generating large images with seamless, consistent style.
